## Production Image Guidelines

### For Production Deployment

When deploying to production, consider these enhancements:

1. **Licensed Photography**
   - Purchase exclusive licenses from Getty Images, Shutterstock, or Adobe Stock
   - Budget: $50-200 per image for extended commercial licenses
   - Benefit: Legal protection, exclusive rights, higher quality

2. **Custom Photography**
   - Commission professional photographer for authentic student/university photos
   - Budget: $1,500-5,000 for professional photo shoot
   - Benefit: Unique, brand-aligned imagery

3. **Official Partnerships**
   - Contact Federal Student Aid for official logo usage permission
   - Partner with universities for authentic campus imagery
   - Obtain written agreements for testimonial photos

4. **Model Releases**
   - Ensure all individuals shown in photos have signed model releases
   - Required for testimonial sections and marketing materials
   - Protects against privacy/publicity rights issues

### Image Optimization

All production images should be:
- **Optimized**: Use WebP format with JPEG fallback
- **Responsive**: Multiple sizes for different screen resolutions
- **Lazy-loaded**: Improve page load performance
- **Alt text**: Comprehensive descriptions for accessibility
- **CDN-hosted**: Fast delivery via Content Delivery Network

### Recommended Services
- **Unsplash** (Free): Current solution, perfect for prototypes
- **Adobe Stock** ($29.99/mo): Professional licensed images
- **Getty Images** (Custom pricing): Premium exclusive content
- **Shutterstock** ($29/mo): Large selection, good value
